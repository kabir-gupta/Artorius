ARTORIUS

We are Artorius. A start-up dedicated to helping artists of dying art forms.

What we do

Around the world, there are thousands of artists who are skilled in various ethnic arts. Unfortunately, many of these art forms are dying as their audience size grows smaller. They make well below the minimum wage.

Artorius contacts various artists of such art forms and sell their artwork. They in turn get part of the profits from their artwork that is sold.

Of Course we aren't just framing them. We are also imprinting it onto various accessories. Suitcases, handbags, you name it!

Doing this, we are aiming to bring back these dying art forms and make them more popular.

About us!

Artorius was founded in 2018 by 3 students. It was originally a startup created to participate in an entrepreneurship contest known as the Young Founder's Summit by Smarter Me. We were based in Stamford American International School in Singapore before we disbanded.
